Usage Rules
RuleDetail
One Primary per viewOnly one .btn-cancel (green) primary CTA per screen section
Button orderPrimary (green) always on the right; Cancel/Reset on the left
No red buttonsRed is reserved for error states and alerts, never buttons
Icons in buttonsUse Material Icons at font-size: 15–16px with gap: .375rem
Disabled stateAdd disabled attribute; CSS applies opacity: .6; cursor: not-allowed
Full-width on mobileAdd w-100 or justify-content: center below 576px
